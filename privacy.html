Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

Vendetta Bot collects the following information:

  • Discord User ID: Used to identify your account and link it to your Roblox profile.
  • Roblox User ID and Username: Used to display your Roblox presence and receive help requests.
  • Help Request Data: Information you provide when requesting help, including server links, targets, and optional images.

How We Use Your Information

  • Display your Roblox online/offline status in Discord
  • Send help requests to available members
  • Improve bot functionality and user experience
  • Debug and troubleshoot issues

Data Storage

All data is stored securely in an encrypted SQLite database. Images uploaded are stored temporarily and are only accessible through Discord's CDN.

We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law.

Your Rights

You can request to have your data deleted by contacting a server administrator.

Contact

For any privacy concerns, please contact the server administrator or bot owner.
